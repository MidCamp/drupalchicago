Chicago Drupal Meetups

Drupal Chicago
Holds two meetups a month in Chicago, one with presentations, one with an open, discussion format. Drupal Sprints are scheduled throughout the year.
Fox Valley Drupal
This very active meetup groups holds different type of meetups that include presentation, co-working lab time, and social events.
Northwest Drupal Meetup Group
This group meets infrequently in Schaumburg, IL with meetups that cover Presentations, Hands-On Coaching, and Q&A discussions.
Village of Oak Park Drupal User Group
The Village of Oak Park Drupal User Group is a family-friendly all-ages meetup group that meets in Oak Park, Illinois whose meetups are sprints.
Chicago Drupal Meet Up Group
The original meetup group in Chicago hosts small training events once or twice a year.
