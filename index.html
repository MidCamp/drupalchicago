Chicago Drupal Meetups

Drupal Chicago
Drupal Chicago holds periodic meetups, with the format varying from presentations to lightning talks to demos to socials. See Drupal Chicago Meetup Group for schedule and location. Drupal Contribution Days and other events are scheduled throughout the year.
Fox Valley Drupal
This very active meetup groups holds different type of meetups that include presentation, co-working lab time, and social events.
 

MidCamp
Midwest Drupal Camp

MidCamp is held annually at DePaul University - Lincoln Park Student Center!

For more information, please visit https://midcamp.org, subscribe to our email newsletter, and follow us on Twitter - @midwestcamp

We hope to see you there!

  • Web: https://midcamp.org
  • Twitter: https://twitter.com/midwestcamp
  • Mastodon: @midcamp@fosstodon.org
  • Hashtag #midcamp
  • Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/MidCamp
